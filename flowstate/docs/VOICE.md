# Flowstate Voice Guide

Audience: programmers, indie hackers, deep-work people. They live in editors and
terminals, smell marketing from a mile away, and respect tools that respect them.

## The voice in one line
A producer who codes, talking to another builder at 1 AM — calm, specific, a little
dry. Premium chill: confident enough to underclaim.

## Rules

1. **Dev-to-dev, not brand-to-consumer.** Write like a README by someone with taste,
   not a landing page by a growth team.
2. **Concrete beats adjective.** "80+ original tracks, produced in-house" — never
   "curated high-fidelity soundtracks". Numbers, nouns, verbs.
3. **Understate.** If a sentence sounds impressive, cut it by a third. No exclamation
   marks. No emoji in product chrome (social captions may keep at most one).
4. **Claims must be checkable or deleted.** Terms say "no productivity claims" —
   the copy obeys the Terms. No "boosts focus by X%", no neuroscience cosplay.
5. **Gen-Z cadence, not gen-z costume.** Short sentences. Fragments fine. Lowercase
   vibes where the design allows. Never: slang that will age ("no cap", "fr"),
   hype-bro ("locked in 🔥", "grind"), or corporate zombie ("elevate", "seamless",
   "unlock", "premium", "supercharge", "journey", "movement", "revolutionize").
6. **The moat is the music.** Every surface may quietly remind: one producer makes
   every track here. That's the story no competitor can copy.

## Indonesian (id locale)
- "aku / kamu" — personal tapi ga alay. Bukan "Anda" (kaku), bukan "gue/lo" (terlalu
  tongkrongan buat UI).
- Jangan terjemahan literal dari EN — tulis ulang dengan ritme Indonesia yang natural.
- Istilah teknis yang lazim di kalangan dev dibiarkan Inggris (deep work, focus,
  session, track) — jangan dipaksa jadi "kerja dalam".

## Calibration examples

| Surface | Slop (before) | Voice (after — EN) | Voice (after — ID) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Hero sub | "A premium Pomodoro timer integrated with original focus soundtracks…" | "A focus timer with music that was actually made for it. Produced in-house, not licensed from a stock library." | "Timer fokus dengan musik yang memang dibuat buat ini. Diproduksi sendiri, bukan ambil dari stock library." |
| Insight msg | "Your focus is solid. Keep up the good work." | "Steady week. The streak's holding." | "Minggu yang stabil. Streak kamu masih jalan." |
| Empty state | "Complete focus sessions with the Pomodoro timer to start tracking…" | "No sessions yet. Run one block — the charts start themselves." | "Belum ada sesi. Jalankan satu block — grafiknya nyusul sendiri." |
| Share caption | "Locked in for 50m deep work! 🧠🔥 Join the deep work movement" | "50 focused minutes on Flowstate. Music by the person who built it." | "50 menit fokus di Flowstate. Musiknya dibuat langsung sama yang bikin app-nya." |

## Hard blacklist
premium, seamless, supercharge, unlock, elevate, journey, movement, revolutionize,
game-changer, effortless, "!" anywhere, emoji in UI chrome, "no cap"/"fr"/"vibes af",
fake precision ("up to 20 minutes of cognitive pollution").
