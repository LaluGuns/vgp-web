Caption:
Latency changes the player body.

Monitoring delays exceeding 10 ms disrupt a performer's physical auditory-motor feedback loop. The player fights the lag, resulting in stiff, rushed timing and a loss of natural performance energy.

The play: Set your DAW buffer to 64 samples or less during tracking. Disable all high-latency master bus plugins to reduce round-trip delay below 10 ms.
