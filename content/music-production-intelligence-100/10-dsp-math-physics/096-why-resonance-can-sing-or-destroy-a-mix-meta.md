Caption:
Resonance can sing or wreck the mix.

Sweeping with a narrow EQ boost to hunt for resonances leads directly to over-filtering. You identify normal transient peaks as offensive, notches them out, and strip the life out of your acoustic tracks.

The play: Use dynamic EQ or multiband compression instead of static notches. This attenuates harsh resonant peaks only when they exceed the threshold, keeping the fundamental wood and body intact.
