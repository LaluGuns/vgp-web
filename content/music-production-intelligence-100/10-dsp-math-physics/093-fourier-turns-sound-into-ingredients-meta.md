The Fourier transform decomposes complex time-domain acoustic waves into individual frequency sine waves, showing you the exact amplitude of each block. But mixing visually causes you to chase curves instead of sound.

The play: Set your spectrum analyzer window size to 4096 samples with a Hann window. This balances low-frequency resolution with transient response for precise troubleshooting.
