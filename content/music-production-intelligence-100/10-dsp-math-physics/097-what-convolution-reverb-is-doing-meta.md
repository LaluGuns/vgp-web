Convolution reverb copies a space.

Algorithmic reverbs use digital delay lines that can sound metallic and artificial. They fail to capture the complex, randomized early reflections and frequency absorption of a real physical space.

The play: Load your convolution reverb on a send channel. Insert a high-pass filter at 150 Hz before the reverb, and adjust the pre-delay to separate dry vocal transients from the early reflections.
