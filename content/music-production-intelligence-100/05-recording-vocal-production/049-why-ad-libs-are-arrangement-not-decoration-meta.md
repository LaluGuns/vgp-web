Caption:
Ad-libs are arrangement moves, not decoration.

Placing ad-libs in the center of the stereo image causes them to compete with the lead vocal. This frequency overlap splits the listener's focus, making the entire mix feel chaotic and cluttered.

To fix this, pan the ad-libs wide and apply aggressive high-pass and low-pass filters. This carves out a separate frequency and spatial pocket, allowing the ad-libs to support the mix without masking the lead.
