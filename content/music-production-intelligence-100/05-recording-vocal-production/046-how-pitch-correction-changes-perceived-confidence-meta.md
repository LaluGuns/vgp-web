Description: Aggressive pitch correction strips the natural emotion and perceived confidence out of a vocal. This guide breaks down the science of pitch perception and provides a session move to set retune speed for believable performances.

Caption:
Over-tuning a vocal removes the singer's confidence.

Perfect pitch correction flattens the micro-tuning variations that human ears associate with emotional confidence. When you lock a vocal to a grid, you remove the natural pitch glides that convey effort and passion.

To fix this, use manual pitch correction tools with a slow retune speed. Focus on correcting only the off-key notes while leaving the natural vibrato and micro-glides intact.
