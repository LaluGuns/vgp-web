## Caption
While vowels carry the pitch, consonants carry the attitude. Aggressive de-essing and heavy compression strip away these high-frequency transient bursts, leaving vocals sounding dull and slurred. Instead of relying on global plugins, use volume automation to tame sibilance. This preserves the natural impact and emotional edge of the words. Save this post for your next vocal session. Follow @virzyguns and visit www.virzyguns.com for daily session science.
