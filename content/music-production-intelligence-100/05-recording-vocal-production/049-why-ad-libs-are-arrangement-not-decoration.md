## Caption
Ad-libs are not just background decoration; they are critical arrangement decisions. When they overlap the lead vocal or compete for space, they clutter the listener's focus. By applying auditory stream segregation, you can space ad-libs so they sit cleanly in the gaps. This creates a solid call-and-response dynamic that keeps the listener engaged. Try muting any ad-libs that compete with the lead to feel the difference. Save this post for your next mix. Follow @virzyguns and visit www.virzyguns.com for daily session science.

## Pinned comment
Arrangement tip: Pan your ad-libs hard left and right, and use a heavy reverb or delay. This physically separates them from the dry, center-focused lead vocal stream. Save this post and follow for more.
