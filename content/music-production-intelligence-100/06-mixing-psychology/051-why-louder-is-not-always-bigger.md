## Caption
The oldest trick in audio is that louder sounds better. When you add a plugin and it boosts the level, your brain thinks it improved the sound. In reality, it might just be louder and harsher. By level-matching your inputs and outputs, you remove this bias and hear the true effect of your processing. Next time you tweak a compressor or EQ, match the volumes to see if you actually improved the mix. Save this post before touching your next fader.
