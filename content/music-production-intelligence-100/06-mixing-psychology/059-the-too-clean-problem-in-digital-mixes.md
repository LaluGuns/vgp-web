## Caption
A clinically clean digital mix is often a boring mix.

When you remove all noise, dynamic fluctuations, and minor harmonic distortion, you strip away the natural friction that makes music feel physical. Tape machine simulation and tube saturation introduce the organic textures that listeners connect with.

The Session Move:
1. Insert a tape saturation plugin on your main drum and vocal buses.
2. Dial in the drive until you can just hear the compression and thickening.
3. Level match the output fader of the plugin so the volume does not fool your ears.

Toggle the effect on and off in the context of the full track. If the song suddenly feels warmer and more alive, you have given it a human edge.
