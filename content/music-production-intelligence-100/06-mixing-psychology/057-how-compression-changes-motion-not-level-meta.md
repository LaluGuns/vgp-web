Title: Compression changes motion before level

Description: Understand how compression changes the movement and rhythm of your tracks rather than just controlling peak levels. Learn to set attack and release times to sharpen the groove of your mix.

Hashtags: #VGP #MixingTips #AudioScience #HomeStudio #MusicProduction

CTA: Save this before touching another fader.

Caption:
Compression is a motion designer, not a volume knob.

Using compression just to control levels squashes the transient peaks and ruins the rhythmic pulse. A compressor shapes the attack and sustain envelopes, which directly dictates how a track grooves.

To fix this, set the compressor release time to pump in time with the song's tempo. Make sure the gain reduction meter returns to zero right before the next transient hit to keep the motion alive.
