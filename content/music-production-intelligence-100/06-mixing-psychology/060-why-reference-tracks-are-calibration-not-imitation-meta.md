Caption:
Reference tracks are for calibration, not imitation.

Trying to copy the exact EQ curve of a commercial release leads to phase issues and unbalanced mixes. Your ears adapt to frequencies over time, causing you to lose perspective during long sessions.

To fix this, level-match the reference track to your mix using an integrated loudness meter. Use it to check your low-end levels and high-end brightness, resetting your hearing baseline.
