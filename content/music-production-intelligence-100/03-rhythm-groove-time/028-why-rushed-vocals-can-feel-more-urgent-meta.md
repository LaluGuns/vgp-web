Title: Rushed vocals sound urgent

Description: Why rushed vocals feel more urgent. Speech prosody timing communicates feeling.

Caption:
Locked vocal timing lacks passion.

Dragging every vocal phrase to match the beat grid perfectly removes performance intensity. The brain decodes vocal transients ahead of the beat as excitement or urgency, and late vocals as relaxed.

Nudge the vocal track forward by 10 to 25 milliseconds relative to the backing track. Check the shifted vocal in mono to ensure the transient remains clear.
