## Caption
Swing is not random timing looseness. It is math combined with physical expectation. Standard swing templates can sound robotic if they do not match your song tempo. Keep your kick drum straight to ground the beat, then use your ears to set a custom long-short pattern on the hi-hats. Compare straight hats against a swung duplicate to feel the roll. Save this before quantizing your next groove.

## Pinned comment
Session tip: If your swing feels too bouncy, check the tempo. Faster tempos need lower swing percentages to feel natural, while slower tempos can take heavier shuffles.
