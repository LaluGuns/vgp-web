Why does a perfect grid feel so stiff?

Snapping every hit to mathematical perfection kills the natural swing. Human feel lives in tiny timing offsets that help the brain group auditory streams.

Lock your kick and snare to hold the foundation, then nudge your hi-hats late or early by 5 to 15 milliseconds. Listen to the track from across the room. If it makes you nod your head, keep the move.
