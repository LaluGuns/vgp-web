Caption:
The grid is not the groove.

Quantizing all parts to a grid eliminates the microtiming offsets that define stylistic pocket. The brain reacts to absolute timing precision as unnatural, losing interest when expectation matching becomes too easy.

Lock the kick drum, then play or slide the snare slightly behind the grid by 12 milliseconds. Compare a fully quantized group against one with offset snare and hats, checking for physical body movement.
