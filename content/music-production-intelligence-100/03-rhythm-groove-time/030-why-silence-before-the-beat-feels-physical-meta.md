Title: Silence before the beat creates physical impact

Description: Why silence before the beat feels physical. Clearing sound before the beat resets the auditory threshold.

Caption:
Constant sound weakens the drop.

Letting tails and build-ups bleed directly into the drop soft-focuses the transient impact. Clearing sound before the beat allows the ear's dynamic range to reset, maximizing the perceived loudness of the drop.

Slice all audio and MIDI tracks 50 milliseconds before the downbeat of the drop. Compare the drop entry with and without the silence gap to hear if the silence adds physical impact.
