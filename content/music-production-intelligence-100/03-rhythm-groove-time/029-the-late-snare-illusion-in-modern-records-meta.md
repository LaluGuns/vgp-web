Title: The late snare creates weight

Description: Why the late snare illusion works in modern records. Snapping the snare directly to the center can feel rushed.

Hashtags: #VGP #Groove #BeatMaking #MusicProduction #Rhythm

CTA: Save this before quantizing your snare

Caption:
Perfect snare timing feels lightweight.

Snapping the snare drum to the exact grid downbeat can make the backbeat sound small. Delaying the snare transient by 10 to 25 milliseconds prevents it from masking the kick, keeping both punches clear.

Nudge the snare track late by 12 to 20 milliseconds while keeping the kick locked. Toggle between the grid-locked snare and the delayed snare, listening for changes in groove weight.
