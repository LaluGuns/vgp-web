## Caption
Dragging every vocal phrase to match the beat grid perfectly removes the human performance intensity. Listeners decode rushed timing as passion, excitement, or urgency. Let the vocal lead the beat in emotional passages, then pull them back in time for the hook. Save this before aligning the vocals.

Session tip: Only nudge vocal phrases that clash with main snare transients. Let the rest of the vocal drift naturally to keep the performance human.
