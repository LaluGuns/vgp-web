## Caption
A great groove sits in the space between the rigid grid and natural human feel. The grid is a helpful guide, not a prison. Snapping every instrument to the same grid lines prevents tracks from interacting. Keep your kick drum locked to the downbeat to anchor the rhythm, then let the bass and hats lean around it. ## Pinned comment
Session tip: Solo your drums and vocal, then clap along to find where the vocal wants to sit. Adjust your percussion to match that pocket.
