## Caption
Why does a perfect grid feel so stiff? It is because human feel lives in tiny timing offsets. Snapping every hit to mathematical perfection kills the natural swing. Lock your kick and snare to hold the foundation, then nudge your hi-hats late or early by 5 to 15 milliseconds. Listen to the track from across the room. If it makes you nod your head, keep the move. Save this before quantizing your next groove.

## Pinned comment
Session tip: Duplicate your hi-hat track. Keep one straight, nudge the other by 8 milliseconds late, and blend them to create a wide microtiming cloud.
