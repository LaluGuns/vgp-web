Title: Syncopation wakes up the listener

Description: Why syncopation shocks the auditory cortex. Use syncopation to break metric expectations.

Caption:
Metric predictability leads to habituation.

Repeating standard downbeat patterns causes the brain to ignore the rhythm, leading to listener fatigue. Dropping an accent where the brain does not expect it creates a tiny spike in attention, triggering reward pathways.

Move a snare or synth stab from the beat 4 downbeat to the preceding 16th-note upbeat. A/B the syncopation against the straight grid, checking if the groove loses its drive.
