Caption:
Chasing a single platform target number can ruin your master.

Targeting minus fourteen LUFS because of website advice often leaves your track sounding thin and weak. Streaming platforms lower loud audio, but they never add dynamics back to a squashed file.

Try this in your next project:
1. Master for real-world impact, translation, and peak safety.
2. Enable volume normalization in your media player when comparing references.
3. Make sure your track holds its own against recent hits. Let the music dictate the density, not the platforms.
