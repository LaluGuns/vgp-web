# Humanizer checklist

Every content file in this folder has to pass this check before use.

- No em dash or en dash.
- Use straight quotes only.
- Keep claims specific and sourced.
- Replace vague authority with a named source.
- Use simple verbs.
- Avoid fake novelty language, ornate importance language, and broad platform promises.
- Avoid generic motivational endings.
- Keep slide text readable in about 3 seconds.
- Prefer concrete production moves over abstract advice.
- Mark each science claim as source-backed, producer interpretation, safe analogy, or avoid exact claim.
- Every content file needs a CTA field. Slide 10 must use the topic-specific CTA as its headline.
- No support line may be identical across files.
- Description, Caption, and Pinned comment must not follow a fill-in-the-blank template.
- Every prompt needs headline and support accent phrases, a platform-safe layout block, and one motion cue.
- Every reference line needs source years or an access year.
- Final file must say: Humanizer status: passed.

- Image prompts may include plain text `www.virzyguns.com` and `100% Art. 100% Science.` when listed in Visible text.
- No image prompt may request or allow a logo, V icon, V mark, monogram, emblem, badge, watermark symbol, handle symbol, or invented brand mark.
- Prompt visible text must match the Visible text list exactly.
