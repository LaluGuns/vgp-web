Title: Noise breathing makes synths alive

Description: Why noise can make synths feel alive. Stochastic variation breaks periodic patterns.

Caption:
Clean digital synths sound sterile.

Generating pure digital waveforms without noise creates a static sound that lacks organic movement. Adding low-level noise introduces stochastic variation to the waveform, preventing the brain from predicting the sound.

Route an envelope generator to the noise channel, letting it decay quickly to blend with the transient. Compare the synth with and without noise.
