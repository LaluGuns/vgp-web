Frequency leveling removes identity.

Using aggressive EQ to match a generic curve removes the unique harmonic peaks that define the sound's character. The ear recognizes sounds based on spectral envelopes and the ratios between the fundamental frequency and upper partials.

Use a narrow EQ boost on the key harmonic signature of a vocal or synth to highlight its unique tone. Verify the sound remains recognizable on small speakers.
