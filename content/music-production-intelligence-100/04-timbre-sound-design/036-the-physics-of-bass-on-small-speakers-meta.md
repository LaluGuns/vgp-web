Caption:
Sub-bass disappears on small screens.

Relying on fundamental sub-bass frequencies below 60 Hz makes the bass line silent on small speakers. The ear hears upper harmonics and reconstructs the missing fundamental pitch, creating the illusion of bass.

Set up a parallel distortion send, bandpass it between 200 Hz and 800 Hz, and blend it with the sub-bass. Play the mix through a smartphone speaker to check translation.
