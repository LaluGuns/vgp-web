Title: Saturation adds size without volume

Description: How distortion creates size without volume. Saturation compresses peaks and adds harmonics.

Hashtags: #VGP #SoundDesign #Synth #MusicProduction #AudioEngineering

CTA: Save this before pushing another fader

Caption:
Loudness chasing ruins dynamic punch.

Relying on volume faders or limiters to make a track sound bigger clips transient peaks and flattens the mix. Soft clipping tames transient peaks while adding odd and even harmonics, raising the average RMS level.

Insert a tape saturation plugin on the drum bus, reducing transient peaks by 2 dB to add size. Compare saturated tracks at matched volume.
