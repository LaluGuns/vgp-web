## Caption
Pushing clean faders ruins your mix balance and clips headroom. Saturation is the secret to making sounds feel larger without raising the volume. Adding nonlinear harmonics fills frequency gaps, making thin tracks feel closer and heavier. Saturate your mid-range instruments, then level-match to compare. Save this before pushing another fader.

## Pinned comment
Session tip: Level match your distorted track against the clean version. If the saturated version does not sound denser at the exact same level, pull the drive back.
