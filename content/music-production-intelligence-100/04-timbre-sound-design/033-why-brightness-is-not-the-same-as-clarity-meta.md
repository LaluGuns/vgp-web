Title: Brightness is not clarity

Description: Why brightness is not the same as clarity. Clarity lives in the mid-range.

Hashtags: #VGP #SoundDesign #Synth #MusicProduction #AudioEngineering

CTA: Save this before boosting your high end

Caption:
Boosting highs creates harshness.

Using a high-shelf boost to fix a dull mix builds high-frequency clutter that causes listener ear fatigue. Intelligibility depends on transient definition and clear mid-range harmonics, not boosted high-shelf frequencies.

Apply a wide 2 dB cut between 200 Hz and 400 Hz on muddy tracks, exposing high end clarity. Compare the mid-range cut with a high boost to see which is cleaner.
