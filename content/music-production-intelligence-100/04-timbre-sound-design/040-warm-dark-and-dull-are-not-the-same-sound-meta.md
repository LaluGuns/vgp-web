Title: Warm is not dark. Dark is not dull

Description: Warm, dark, and dull are not the same sound. Spectral slope dictates sound warmth.

CTA: Save this before filtering your highs

Caption:
Low pass filtering ruins definition.

Using a steep low-pass filter to make a sound warm removes transient definition, making the track dull. Warmth is a gentle high-frequency roll-off combined with rich low-mid harmonics, not a sudden frequency cutoff.

Keep the high-frequency transients clear while using saturation to build the warm low-mid harmonics. Build warmth with saturation rather than steep low-pass filters.
