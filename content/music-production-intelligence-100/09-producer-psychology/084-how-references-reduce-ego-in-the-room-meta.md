Caption:
Auditory accommodation will trick your ears.

Within minutes of mixing, your brain adapts to the frequency balance of your session. A track that feels warm in isolation often sounds muddy when compared to commercial releases.

The play: Use a level-matched reference track to recalibrate your ears every thirty minutes. Bypassing loudness bias is the only way to hear frequency balance.
