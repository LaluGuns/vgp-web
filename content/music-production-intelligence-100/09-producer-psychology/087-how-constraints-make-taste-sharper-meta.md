Caption:
Constraints make taste sharper.

Infinite options dilute your focus. Choice paralysis leads to cluttered arrangements that rely on dense layers to mask bad production decisions.

The play: Set a strict five-sound limit. Build your track using only a kick, snare, bass, lead instrument, and lead vocal before adding any details.
