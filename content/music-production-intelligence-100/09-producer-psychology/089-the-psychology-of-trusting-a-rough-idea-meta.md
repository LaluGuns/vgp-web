Caption:
Rough ideas need protection.

Over-editing destroys the micro-timing variations and transient dynamics that make a performance feel human. A mathematically perfect track often lacks groove and emotion.

The play: Keep your original rough mix as a reference track in the session. Switch to it frequently to ensure your cleanup passes have not stripped away the energy.
