## Caption
Unlimited choices do not make you more creative. In fact, they usually do the opposite.

When you have access to thousands of presets and plugins, you spend your time browsing instead of committing. You stack track layer upon track layer to hide the fact that the core melody is weak.

The play: Set a strict constraint. Try building your next hook using only five sounds. No extra layers, no filler tracks. Just five core channels balanced to perfection.

If it does not work with five sounds, adding a sixth will not fix it. Save this to simplify your production process tonight. Follow @virzyguns and visit www.virzyguns.com for daily session science.
