Caption:
Don't let editing kill your creative flow.

Stopping to quantize midi notes or tune vocals during creation splits your focus. Task-switching forces your brain to dump working memory, which destroys the emotional momentum of your track.

The play: Separate your tracking and editing sessions. Focus entirely on capturing energy today, and leave the corrections for tomorrow.
