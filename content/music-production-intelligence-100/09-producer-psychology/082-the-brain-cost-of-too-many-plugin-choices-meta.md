Caption:
Staring at a menu of a thousand plugins is ruining your workflow. 

Choice overload is a real psychological roadblock. Having too many options leads directly to decision paralysis. Instead of listening to the music, you spend your energy A/B testing digital interfaces that sound almost identical.

The play: Set a hard constraint. Choose just one EQ, one compressor, and one meter for your next mixing pass. Lock the rest away.
